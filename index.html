Mercury

Approximate Diameter: 3032 mi aka 16008392 ft
Fun Fact: It has the shortest year in the solar system, orbiting the Sun in just 88 Earth days.

Venus

Approximate Diameter: 7521 mi aka 39710781 ft
Fun Fact: Venus is the hottest planet in the solar system

Earth

Approximate Diameter: 7926 mi aka 41849176 ft
Fun Fact: only known planet with life

Mars

Approximate Diameter: 4212 mi aka 22239305 ft
Fun Fact: Home to Olympus Mons, the largest volcano in the solar system, which is three times higher than Mount Everest.

Jupiter

Approximate Diameter: 86881 mi aka 458730540 ft
Fun Fact: Jupiter has more than double the mass of all other planets combined.

Saturn

Approximate Diameter: 72367 mi aka 382096810 ft
Fun Fact: Saturn has the most officially recognized moons in the solar system.

Uranus

Approximate Diameter: 31518 mi aka 166414626 ft
Fun Fact: Uranus spins on its side, likely due to a massive collision early in its history.
Neptune

Approximate Diameter: 30775 mi aka 162491596 ft
Fun Fact: Neptune has the fastest recorded wind speeds in the solar system.
